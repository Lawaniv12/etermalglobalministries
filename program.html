Teens Bible School (Hos.4:6)

This is platform for the teenagers between the age of 13 - 17 years in order to have access to the word of God and God's kingdomand to search for uncommon wisdom of God

Aim
  • 1. To teach and assist teenagers to grow spiritually, morally and academically
  • 2. To assist teenagers in developing Godly character
  • 3. To help them discover their purpose early in life
  • 4. To assist them to memorize the word of God in order to stand against devil's schemes
  • 5. To assist them to understand the important of staying pure.

Born to Win leadership training (Young Adults)

A great platform of equipping for young adults between the age of 18 - 22 (Hos.4:6)

Aim
  • 1. To assist young adults to discover their identity and calling in order to serve effectively in their local churches.
  • 2. To teach and equip them to be rooted in Christ
  • 3. To assist them to discover and develop their leadership abilities.
  • 4. To raise world class Christian leaders to affect their society positively.

Amazing Parent Forum (Luk 1:17)

We strive to raise Godly homes and make them ready to meet Jesus.

Aim
  • 1. Raising Godly parents
  • 2. To turn the heart of parents to their children.

Eternal Purpose Ministry (Eph.4:12)

This is a place for interaction and fellowship for believers for their spiritual growth.

Aim
  • 1. To do the work of ministry in the lives of believers.
  • 2. To disciple believers to discover and fulfil their calling
